incident-retro · post-incident review

Turn a resolved incident into a blameless RCA.

The reflection step of the support loop. Give it what happened and the numbers, and it builds the review a senior engineer or business analyst is expected to produce: a timeline, a computed blast radius, a 5-Whys chain, contributing factors, and three prioritised actions. The app owns every number; the wording is yours or an optional draft you review.
